about us
STAR TOURS
Star Tours was established in January 2019 by two Zanzibaris. It is a Zanzibar based tour operator specializing in organizing excursions in Zanzibar Island. As a service provider, we provide all services correlated to tourist needs within our area of operation, you can expect the highest quality service, that comes with the best range of tours in Zanzibar.
Our Vision and Values
From greeting to goodbye, our vision is to create inspiring experiences for every guest and every stage of their journey with us. We do our very best to provide the best customer care in the industry. In doing so, we always remember the little details when it comes to making sure everyone’s experience shines.
Trust & Respect
We treat each other as we like to be treated ourselves and we trust each other and do the right thing, that means providing the best experience to our guests, keeping every interaction positive and doing what we say we will do.
Fun & Passion
We are completely passionate about what we do, we embrace bright concepts and bring fun and laughter to our experiences with guests and staffs. We always strive for excellence and reliable service in everything we do.
