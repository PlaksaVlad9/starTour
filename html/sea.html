MNEMBA ISLAND
MNEMBA ISLAND TOUR
Mnemba Island is a single small island located about 3 km off the northeast coast of Unguja, the largest island of the Zanzibar Archipelago, Tanzania, opposite Muyuni Beach.
It is roughly triangular in shape, about 500 metres in diameter and about 1.5 kilometres in circumference. It is a popular scuba diving site, with a wide variety of corals gardens and associated species, as well as occasional sightings of larger species such as turtles and dolphins make it be one of the most frequented snorkelling spots in the world.
