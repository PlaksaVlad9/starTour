SAFARI BLUE TOUR
OFF FUMBA ISLAND
SAFARI BLUE TOUR
It is the best opportunity to follow the footpath of sultans, salves and explorers through the narrow, winding alleyways of this magical town.
You will get the chance to visit the Cathedral, Slave Markets, the Sultans Palace, Tip Tip’s House, the Old Arabic Fort, the fruit and vegetable market, Forodhani Park and the famous Zanzibar Doors in the petite streets, you will explore the events that shaped Zanzibar and see where they occurred.
