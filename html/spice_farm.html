SPICE FARM TOUR
ZANZIBAR ISLAND
SPICE FARM TOUR
Uncover the trade that made Zanzibar to be called “The Spice Island.” The guide will show you how familiar spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, saffron and cloves are grown and cultivated.
Once the tour finished, you will get time to buy spices, soaps and fragrance (scent) products which are all handmade by the local community. As a half day trip, this tour can be combined with other tours such as Stone Town, Jozani Forest & Butterfly Centre Tours.
